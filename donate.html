Donate

When you donate you are not only helping us change lives you are also helping us keep everything up and running smoothly. Donating allows us to continue to do our work throughout New Zealand, regardless of age, gender or race!

By donating you automatically go into the draw for prizes when you attend one of our events. You also are able to claim special rewards at any of our fundraising events at the marked locations! All you need to do is show the QR code that was emailed to you or press here to resend email.

What benefits you get:

  • $5 : Free Wristband
  • $10 : Entry into monthly raffle
  • $20 : Free Drinks
  • $50 : Blind + Low Vision T shirt
  • $100+ : Free entry into our paid events + special invites and special notification regarding events

All teirs higher than the previous have the benefits of the previous
